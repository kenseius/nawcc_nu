{{#markdown}} ### What Are Content Layout Components? **Content Layout** is used to arrange the content on a page, and within sections. These components are used for laying out the content within a page or section. All content lives within one of the 6 content containers: - Main - Article - Section - Aside - Header - Footer Then, that content can be presented in a number ways, depending on the type of content, how the content is organized, and much content there is. Either a 1 column or 2 column layout is applied to all content: - Layouts - 1 col - 2 col - Full Screen The content can then be broken down into visual groupings, ans navigated through via secondary or tertiary navigation. - Cards - Heros - Accordions - Tabs - Grids - Media Objects - Tooltips - Tables - Modals - Alerts {{/markdown}}
