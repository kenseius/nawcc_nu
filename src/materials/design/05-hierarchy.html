--- title: Hierarchy subtitle: STRAPLESS - DESIGN icon: fa-th --- {{#markdown}} The basic site hierarchy is as follows: Every page can be either a **parent** or a **child**. **Parents** are any page that links to any number of subpages. **Child** are the bottom of the navigation chain --- in other words, pages that do not have children pages. - Parents must provide navigation to their children.
